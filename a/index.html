Introduction#

Organic gardening is more than just a trend; it’s a lifestyle choice that benefits our health, our environment, and our wallets. In this guide, we’ll explore the ins and outs of organic gardening, from choosing the right seeds to harvesting your crops.

What is Organic Gardening?#

Organic gardening is a method of growing plants without the use of synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. Instead, organic gardeners rely on natural processes and materials to nourish their plants and protect them from pests and diseases.

Why Choose Organic Gardening?#

There are many reasons to choose organic gardening. Not only does it reduce our exposure to harmful chemicals, but it also promotes biodiversity, improves soil health, and helps combat climate change.
